# Investor Readiness Checklist

**Gate before Series A conversations** — evidence, not ARR  

Derived from [Investment Memo](INVESTMENT-MEMO.md).

---

## Product & Architecture

- [x] Doctrine internally consistent — Architecture v1.0 ratified
- [x] Abstraction generalizes — Intent → Mission → Events → History
- [x] Demonstration executable locally
- [ ] Playback demonstrably best-in-class for target user (control room review)

---

## Customer Evidence

- [ ] **One reference customer** — public or investor-verifiable
- [ ] **Hundreds of missions** completed on platform (not demos)
- [ ] **Scorecard Before/After** — consistent improvement
- [ ] **MCI trend** — measurable uplift over 30+ days ([MCI](../evidence/benchmark/MISSION-COORDINATION-INDEX.md))
- [ ] **Operator satisfaction** — qualitative + "would use again"
- [ ] **Independent observer** validation (insurer, municipal, or third party)

---

## Integration & Expansion

- [ ] At least **one partner integration** (RSP adapter or approved simulator path)
- [ ] **Second organization type** on same customer's network
- [ ] **Time to value** — first successful mission within 1 day of training

---

## Commercial & Legal

- [ ] **First paid deployment** — any amount; proves procurement path
- [ ] [Data ownership](DATA-OWNERSHIP.md) terms in customer contract — legal reviewed
- [ ] Economic buyer identified (control room and/or insurer)

---

## Market & Legitimacy

- [ ] **Wave 1 published** — thought leadership only
- [ ] **Wave 2 case study** — Evidence labelled, limitations included
- [ ] Customer Advisory Group active (5–8 operators)
- [ ] AI mentioned sparingly externally; outcomes lead

---

## Monthly Discipline

- [ ] [Monthly Learning Log](MONTHLY-LEARNING-LOG.md) — what real missions taught us

---

## Series A Narrative Ready When

```text
One control room. Hundreds of missions. MCI improved X→Y.
One integration. One public reference. Clear expansion inside customer network.
Data ownership clear. Paid deployment. Wave 2 evidence published.
```

Not: dozens of customers, high ARR, full standards ecosystem published.
