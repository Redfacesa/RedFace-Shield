# North Star

**Company-wide objective — not engineering, not revenue.**

> **Enable independent organizations to coordinate critical operations through verifiable, interoperable workflows.**

Every feature, integration, partnership, and customer conversation should reinforce this.

---

## How to Use It

| Decision | Question |
|----------|----------|
| New feature | Does it help independent orgs coordinate? |
| Integration | Does it improve verifiability or interoperability? |
| Public document | Does it lead with the operational problem? |
| Sales conversation | Coordination and evidence first — not AI |
| Standard (Wave 3) | Does industry already have this interoperability problem? |

---

## What It Is Not

- Not "build more engines"  
- Not "win AI for security"  
- Not "replace all existing software"  
- Not "guarantee trust" — enable **verifiable** coordination  

---

## Language Discipline

Lead with the **operational problem**. Use RedFace vocabulary to explain — don't expect industry to adopt terms overnight.

| Term | Use when |
|------|----------|
| Intent | Explaining declared outcomes |
| Mission | Explaining adaptive orchestration |
| Capability | Explaining matching beyond company names |
| Verifiable operations | Explaining trust from history |

Full vocabulary: internal [Canonical Language](../volume-0.75-ratification/CANONICAL-LANGUAGE.md). Public Wave 1 docs define terms inline.

---

## Relationship to Evidence

**North Star** = direction (Vision)  
**Evidence Repository** = proof (Evidence)  

Never confuse the two in external communications.
