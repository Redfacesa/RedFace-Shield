# Volume 0.75: Non-Goals

**Status:** Draft — Ratification Phase  
**Authority:** Subordinate to [Volume −1](../volume-minus-1-philosophy/PHILOSOPHY.md) and [Axioms](AXIOMS.md)  
**Scope:** What RedFace will never do

---

## Purpose

Great platforms define boundaries. Non-goals make the platform **credible** — they prevent scope creep, misplaced customer expectations, and architectural corruption.

If a proposal violates a non-goal, it is rejected regardless of revenue potential.

---

## Non-Goal 1 — Human Accountability

**RedFace does not replace human accountability.**

AI recommends. Policy permits. Humans decide — unless policy explicitly delegates with full audit. RedFace never autonomously exercises legal authority, use of force, or evidentiary certification without human or policy accountability chain.

---

## Non-Goal 2 — Fabricated Facts

**RedFace does not fabricate operational facts.**

The Intelligence Engine creates hypotheses, not history. Facts enter the system only through verified events (Axiom 8). There is no "AI said it happened" without attestation path.

---

## Non-Goal 3 — Data Proprietorship

**RedFace does not own customer operational data.**

Participants own their data. RedFace holds data in trust for operational coordination. Export, restriction, and revocation are participant rights subject to active mission continuity and legal obligation — not platform discretion.

---

## Non-Goal 4 — Forced Workflows

**RedFace does not force organizations into one workflow.**

Missions are adaptive. Participants retain sovereign systems integrated via RSP. RedFace orchestrates; it does not replace control rooms, dispatch software, or insurer systems.

---

## Non-Goal 5 — Proprietary Hardware

**RedFace does not require proprietary hardware.**

Any verified device, camera, sensor, or system may publish RSP events through RTN identity. "Works with RedFace" is a protocol certification — not a hardware lock-in.

---

## Non-Goal 6 — Universal Legal Framework

**RedFace does not assume one country's legal framework applies everywhere.**

Policy Engine, retention, evidence standards, and compliance adapt per jurisdiction. South African POPIA, sector regulations (PSIRA), and local evidentiary requirements are first-class — not afterthoughts.

---

## Non-Goal 7 — Trust Guarantees

**RedFace does not guarantee trust.**

The platform makes operations verifiable. Trust is earned by participants through history. RedFace computes scores; it does not vouch for participants commercially.

---

## Non-Goal 8 — Operating as a Security Company

**RedFace does not employ guards, operate armed response, sell insurance, or perform recovery.**

RedFace is infrastructure. Competing with participants creates irreconcilable conflict of interest.

---

## Non-Goal 9 — Editable History

**RedFace does not provide editable operational history.**

No admin panel. No "correct history" button. Supersession only (Axiom 6). This non-goal is absolute.

---

## Non-Goal 10 — Surveillance Platform

**RedFace does not build mass surveillance infrastructure.**

Cross-participant intelligence uses policy-governed sharing, aggregated patterns, and mission-scoped visibility — not bulk indiscriminate monitoring. Municipal and government participation requires explicit policy and jurisdictional compliance.

---

## Non-Goal 11 — Single Vertical Lock-In

**RedFace does not architect for security alone.**

Shield is the first reference implementation, not the permanent identity. Kernel primitives must remain vertical-agnostic. Security-specific logic lives in Shield — not in kernel engines.

---

## Non-Goal 12 — Primitive Proliferation

**RedFace does not introduce new foundational primitives without doctrine amendment.**

Fifteen axioms. Eleven named primitives. Closed vocabulary. Feature convenience is not justification for new ontology (Axiom 15).

---

## Using Non-Goals

In design review:

> "This feature lets admins edit dispatch timestamps for billing."

Check Non-Goal 9 (editable history) and Axiom 6. Rejected.

> "We should guarantee participants on the platform are trustworthy."

Check Non-Goal 7. Rejected — compute trust from history; do not guarantee.

> "Let's build our own bodycam hardware."

Check Non-Goal 5. Rejected — certify devices; do not manufacture.
