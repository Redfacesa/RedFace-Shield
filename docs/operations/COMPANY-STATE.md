## First customer is not revenue

The first deployment buys:

- Operational truth
- Credibility
- Reference data
- Repeatable learning
- A partner willing to tell you when you're wrong

Revenue compounds from those assets.

---

## Deployment notebook (every observation)

1. **What slowed the operator down?**
2. **What information did they look for that RedFace didn't immediately provide?**
3. **What did they completely ignore?**

Unused features are evidence too.

---

## Rule

> **Never argue with an operator during a deployment.**

- Misunderstanding → product failed to communicate
- Avoided workflow → workflow needs improvement
- Workaround invented → study it

Reality always wins.

---

## You'll know it's working when

Not *"Nice software."* Not even a purchase.

When an operator says:

- *"Can we use RedFace today?"*
- *"Can you pull up yesterday's replay?"*
- *"Where's the Mission Brief?"*

They're talking about their work — not the technology. That's when software disappears into operations.
