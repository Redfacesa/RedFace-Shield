# DEP-001 — Reference Deployment

**Purpose:** Generate enough operational evidence to decide whether RedFace deserves to exist as an independent company.

**Timeline:** Next 90 days  
**Target:** 50+ missions (ODR-2) · patterns by ~200 missions

---

## The three assumptions (what DEP-001 actually tests)

### Hypothesis A — Operator preference

> Operators **prefer** coordinating work through RedFace — not merely *can* use it.

| Signal | How we measure |
|--------|----------------|
| Preference | Operators choose RedFace over phone/radio/WhatsApp when both available |
| Friction | Voluntary return after first week |
| Negative | Workarounds, parallel tools, "just call me instead" |

**Status:** Untested  
**Evidence:** —

---

### Hypothesis B — Economic improvement

> Organizations measure enough operational improvement to justify paying.

| Signal | How we measure |
|--------|----------------|
| Decision Latency | Intent → first decision (trend ↓) |
| Mission completion | Success rate, duration |
| Replay usage | % missions replayed in review |
| Audit time | Time to produce report / evidence pack |
| Supervisor confidence | Time to Operational Confidence; qualitative quotes |

**Status:** Untested  
**Evidence:** —

---

### Hypothesis C — Product vs. consulting

> The operational model transfers to a second customer with **minimal change**.

| Signal | How we measure |
|--------|----------------|
| DEP-002 similarity | Same Brief → Wall → Replay → Report flow without custom kernel |
| Configuration only | No one-off engine changes per customer |
| Failure mode | DEP-001 succeeds only because we customized everything → consulting, not product |

**Status:** Untested (requires DEP-002)  
**Evidence:** —

---

## Partner profile (not the biggest — the fastest teacher)

- Willing to experiment
- Operationally competent
- Leadership decides quickly
- Enough mission volume
- Open to weekly feedback

**Focus:** Vehicle recovery and coordinated response only.

---

## Sales motion (not "buy our platform")

> Give us one week of your operations. We'll measure them together. At the end of the week, you'll know whether RedFace improves coordination in your environment.
