# Volume 6: The AI Architecture

**Status:** Planned  
**Prerequisites:** Volumes 0–4

---

## Scope (Planned)

- AI as Decision Support System (not autonomous commander)
- AI Chief Operations Officer: daily executive intelligence
- Camera feed summarization: 30 feeds → 5 actionable events
- Dispatch recommendation engine
- Duplicate incident detection across participants
- Alarm prioritization based on false-positive history
- Predictive staffing and resource demand
- Model governance, bias prevention, and human override requirements

## Key Questions This Volume Will Answer

- What decisions can AI make vs. recommend?
- How are AI models trained on multi-participant data with consent?
- What is the AI accountability model when recommendations are wrong?
