# RedFace Operational Benchmark

**Status:** Defined — populate with reference customer data  
**Purpose:** Repeatable before/after measurement for organizations evaluating adoption  

---

## Why a Benchmark

Customers need a repeatable way to answer:

> "Are we actually coordinating better?"

Not subjective satisfaction — **measurable dimensions** comparable across deployments.

---

## Benchmark Dimensions

| Dimension | Definition | How measured |
|-----------|------------|--------------|
| **Mission completion time** | Trigger → terminal outcome | Mission playback timestamps |
| **Coordination steps** | Manual handoffs + system transitions | Observer count per mission |
| **Manual handoffs** | Phone calls, WhatsApp, radio for same fact | Operator log / scorecard |
| **Evidence completeness** | Required evidence items captured / required | Mission audit |
| **Playback completeness** | Significant actions present on timeline | Audit vs observer checklist |
| **Audit readiness** | Independent reconstruction without interviews | Third-party review (yes/no) |
| **Time to value** | Registration → first successful mission | Onboarding log |

---

## Scoring (Per Mission)

Use consistent 1–5 or raw counts — pick one per deployment and stick to it.

### Example raw counts

```text
Coordination steps:     Before 12  →  After 7
Manual phone calls:     Before 8   →  After 2
Evidence completeness:  Before 60% →  After 100%
Time to review:         Before 4h  →  After 20m
```

---

## Organization Self-Assessment

Before adoption, an organization measures baseline over **minimum 10 missions** (or 1 week of operations).

After 30 days on platform, remeasure same mission types.

Publish aggregate in Evidence Repository — not single-mission cherry-picking.
