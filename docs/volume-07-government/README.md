# Volume 7: Government & Public Infrastructure

**Status:** Planned  
**Prerequisites:** Volumes 0–3, RTN

---

## Scope (Planned)

- Municipal operational digital twin
- Police integration model (structured intelligence, not surveillance)
- Traffic camera and public sensor integration
- Emergency management: fire, flood, missing persons, disaster response
- Border monitoring, wildlife conservation, mine safety
- Public event crowd management
- Hospital and school emergency coordination
- Data governance for government participants
- POPIA and regulatory compliance for public sector

## Key Questions This Volume Will Answer

- How do municipalities participate without becoming surveillance states?
- What data can police access vs. what requires warrant?
- How does the city operational twin generate municipal revenue?
