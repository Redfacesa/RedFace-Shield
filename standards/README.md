## North Star

> **Enable independent organizations to coordinate critical operations through verifiable, interoperable workflows.**

Every public document should reinforce this — not product features.

---

## Vision vs Evidence

| Label | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Vision** | What we believe is possible — hypothesis |
| **Evidence** | Measured outcome from a specific deployment — fact |

External documents must never present Vision as Evidence.
